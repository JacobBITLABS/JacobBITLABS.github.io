I am a master's student at the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science (IMADA) at the University of Southern Denmark, SDU. Currently, I am working on my master's thesis 'Object Detection and Tracking using fully CNN networks and Transformers'. This mainly with the interest in aerial imagery. The project involves the creation of a dataset and how we can utilize this dataset in existing and future algorithms.
My main objective is to investigate how we can use transformers in the domain of small object detection (tiny objects) and how we can improve the current state.
